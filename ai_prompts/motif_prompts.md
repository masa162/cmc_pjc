# AI Visual Prompts for Asagiri City

This file contains detailed prompts for generating visuals of the various motifs in Asagiri City. These are optimized for high-end image generators like Midjourney v6, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion XL.

## Common Aesthetic Style (Global Prompt Modifier)
> **Style:** Cyberpunk Dystopia, Cinematographic lighting, Neo-noir, Highly detailed, 8k resolution, Unreal Engine 5 render style.
> **Atmosphere:** Rainy, foggy, neon-drenched, industrial, oppressive yet beautiful.

---

### 1. Data-Eater (データ噬み)
**Description:** Bioluminescent fungi/moss eating electronic waste in the Slums (Den-ku).
**Prompt:**
> A close-up cinematic shot of bioluminescent cyan-blue glowing moss and fungi growing over a pile of rusted electronic waste, tangled black data cables, and broken computer chips in a dark, damp cyberpunk slum environment. The fungi occasionally pulse with digital light, projecting faint, flickering holographic fragments of old data into the humid air. Atmospheric fog, macro photography, shallow depth of field, neon highlights.

### 2. Analog Cult (アナログ・カルト)
**Description:** A secret community obsessed with 20th-century technology.
**Prompt:**
> An underground secret hideout filled with stacks of vintage 20th-century technology: CRTs, cassette tapes, film cameras, and patch cables. A group of people in tech-wear are gathered around a glowing vacuum tube amplifier as if performing a ritual. The room is dimly lit by the warm amber glow of old electronics and flickering CRT monitors. Muffled shadows, dusty atmosphere, nostalgic yet futuristic noir.

### 3. Cyber Jizo (電脳地蔵)
**Description:** Small server shrines in the street corner for AI blessings.
**Prompt:**
> A small, weathered stone Jizo statue modified with futuristic metallic plating and glowing LED circuits, tucked into a dark, rainy cyberpunk alley corner. Red bib around its neck, small antennas protruding from its head. Pedestrians are tapping their wrists (cybernetic interfaces) against a glowing terminal at the base of the statue to "offer" data packets. Raindrops reflecting neon lights, urban Shinto-futurism.

### 4. Weather Trading (気象取引)
**Description:** Illegal data trading of real weather experiences.
**Prompt:**
> A high-end, luxury minimalist penthouse in the Upper City. A wealthy socialite is wearing a sleek VR visor, experiencing a hyper-realistic thunderstorm indoors. Rain and lightning flashes are projected as high-fidelity holograms throughout the room, while the actual city outside remains perfectly controlled and dry. A data-chip labeled "STORM DATA" sits on a glass table. Contrast between cold luxury and artificial nature.

### 5. Real Noodle (リアル麺)
**Description:** Rare, real wheat ramen served in the slums.
**Prompt:**
> A steaming bowl of authentic, hand-made ramen with thick noodles and rich broth, served in a chipped ceramic bowl. The setting is a dark, gritty stall in the neon-lit cyberpunk slums. Contrast between the warm, inviting steam of the food and the cold, mechanical surroundings. A character in a worn jacket is leaning over the bowl, steam fogging their cybernetic eyes. Cinematic food photography style.

### 6. Glitch Art (グリッチアート)
**Description:** Hacker-vandalized holographic advertisements.
**Prompt:**
> A massive holographic billboard over a skyscraper street, originally showing a perfect corporate model, now violently distorted with vibrant glitch effects, pixel sorting, and chromatic aberration. Symbols of rebellion and chaotic digital patterns overlap the corporate logo. The distorted light bathes the rainy street below in erratic, flashing neon colors. High energy, digital vandalism.

### 7. Memory Black Market (記憶の闇市)
**Description:** Selling experiences and memories as data.
**Prompt:**
> A dark, illicit market stall in a crowded cyberpunk bazaar. Rows of glowing glass vials or data cartridges labeled with emotive icons (a heart, a sunset, a sword). A customer is having a neural cable connected to their temple by a mysterious dealer. Ethereal, translucent holographic memories of a happy child or a distant ocean are floating briefly in the air between them. Moody lighting, tech-noir.

### 8. Yodohibiki (廃材楽器「澱響」)
**Description:** Industrial instruments made from scrap metal.
**Prompt:**
> A street musician in the slums playing a complex, DIY musical instrument made from rusted pipes, metal plates, and salvaged electronic synthesizers. The instrument has a chaotic, industrial design. The musician is striking a metal plate, creating a visible wave of "noise" light. The background is a cluster of scrap metal and neon cables. Gritty texture, cinematic expressionism.

### 9. Drone Lantern Festival (ドローン灯籠流し)
**Description:** A festival where drones carry lights to honor the erased.
**Prompt:**
> Thousands of small, hand-made drones with warm orange LED lanterns ascending into the dark, smoggy sky of a cyberpunk city. The drones are rising from a dark river in the slums toward the gleaming bottom of the Mid-City above. The golden glow of the lanterns reflects in the wet surfaces of the slums, creating a sense of tragic beauty and hope. Wide angle, epic scale.
